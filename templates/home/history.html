Cameroon Protestant college Bali is an educational establishment founded by the Basel Mission in 1949, but currently owned & run by the Presbyterian Mission in Cameroon.

Essentially imparts general education to students of both sexes (aged 10-19years)& prepares them for the Cameroon GCE exams.
